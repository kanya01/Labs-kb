# Project Summary — live.o

> Source: [docs/design/PROJECT_SUMMARY.md](https://github.com/kanya01/COLABS/blob/main/docs/design/PROJECT_SUMMARY.md)

High-level summary of the live.o music collaboration marketplace platform.

---

## What is live.o?

live.o is a marketplace platform for musicians, producers, and audio engineers to:
- **Discover** each other through a unified `/discover` page with role and skill filters
- **Offer services** with tiered pricing (Basic / Standard / Premium)
- **Transact** securely via Stripe with buyer/seller protections and a 14-day payout hold
- **Collaborate** on orders with a structured lifecycle, revision system, and file delivery
- **Communicate** directly through a built-in messaging system

---

## Core User Roles

- **Producer** — beats, instrumentals, arrangements
- **Vocalist** — vocals, harmonies, session singing
- **Engineer** — mixing, mastering, recording

---

## Platform Differentiators

- Music-specific discovery with portfolio previews and audio playback directly on user cards
- Tiered service model mirrors industry norms (basic mix / standard mix + stems / premium full session)
- End-to-end order management from payment through delivery and revision approval
